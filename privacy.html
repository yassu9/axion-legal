Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Data Collection

We believe in data minimization. We only collect essential information required for the Bot's functionality:

  • Identifiers: Discord User ID, Server ID, and Channel IDs.
  • Configuration: Settings you explicitly configure (e.g., welcome messages, roles).
  • Usage Logs: Anonymized command usage statistics for performance monitoring.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

Your privacy is paramount. We strictly do NOT collect or store:

  • Private Messages (DMs) or message content (unless strictly required for a specific command execution).
  • Passwords, Authentication Tokens, or personal credentials.
  • Payment Details (Credit Card numbers, Bank info). Financial data is handled exclusively by stripe/Razorpay.

3. Data Usage & Sharing

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service.
  • Process premium subscriptions via third-party providers.
  • Detect and prevent technical issues or abuse.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("Right to accept forget").

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

5. Security

We adhere to industry standards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information.
